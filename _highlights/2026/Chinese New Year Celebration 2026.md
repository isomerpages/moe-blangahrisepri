<h4><strong>Chinese New Year Celebration 2026</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 70%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2026 Photo Album/CNY/20260129_134537.jpg">

<p>Our school community came together for a vibrant and meaningful Chinese
New Year celebration, warmly welcoming our senior guests from the Telok
Blangah community and parents from our Parent Support Group. The day began
with students decorating their classrooms and brightening the school with
festive cheer, alongside beautiful decorations by our PSG parents and heartfelt
artwork contributed by our senior guests - a true reflection of our strong
home-school-community partnership.</p>

<p>The concert featured well-wishes from our school leaders, a lively Primary
2 sing-and-dance, and an engaging Primary 4 skit on the legend of Nian
and the significance of red during the festive season. Students also enjoyed
an interactive quiz and a mass dance session before presenting mandarin
oranges to our seniors, teachers and parents as a gesture of respect and
appreciation. The celebration was further enlivened by an energetic lion
dance performance and concluded with community singing and festive greetings,
as we ushered in the new year with kindness, gratitude and togetherness.</p>
