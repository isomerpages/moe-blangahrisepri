<p>We are proud to announce that our students have achieved outstanding results
in the following Science competitions this year:</p>
<p><strong>Singapore Primary Science Olympiad (SPSO) </strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2024 Photos/Science Award/S1.jpg">
</div>
<p>This annual national competition is organized by the NUS High School of
Mathematics and Science. It aims to promote active interest in Science
among primary school students and nurture young Science talents. This year,
the competition comprised a theory paper taken at the participants' respective
primary schools. Our students Muthurasu Thanushraj, Lien Heidi Zhao Hui,
and Teo Kai Xiang have all been awarded Merit Awards for their participation
in the SPSO!</p>
<p><strong>Raffles Science Olympiad (RSO)</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2024 Photos/Science Award/S2.jpg">
</div>
<p>Muthurasu Thanushraj has also won a Merit Award in the Raffles Science
Olympiad (RSO). This competition provides a platform for students to demonstrate
their knowledge and understanding of Science. It consists of two rounds:
an online individual round with a multiple-choice test in various science
subjects, followed by an in-person round for the top performers.</p>
<p><strong>Innovation Programme (IvP)</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2024 Photos/Science Award/S3.jpg">
</div>
<p>Joanne Lee Jia Rong, Vo Catherine Tu, and Phua Ser Yen have been recognised
for their achievements in the Innovation Programme, each receiving a Merit
Award. This program challenges Primary 5 students to develop innovative
solutions to real-world problems in three categories: Daily Journey, Better
with Less, and Home Fix. Students explore data, frame problems, generate
and evaluate ideas, create prototypes, and test their solutions.</p>
<p><strong>The School Science Department's Dedication</strong>
</p>
<p>The Science Department at Blangah Rise Primary School is committed to
nurturing our students' love for science and helping them excel in science
competitions. Our teachers provide students with various resources and
support them in their preparation.</p>
<p>We are proud of our students' achievements and their dedication to excellence
in Science. These awards are a testament to their hard work and perseverance
throughout the competition season. Congratulations once again to our students!</p>
