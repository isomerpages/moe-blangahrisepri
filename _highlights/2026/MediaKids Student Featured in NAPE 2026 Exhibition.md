<p>Congratulations to our MediaKids CCA students - Kayleigh from Primary
6 Topaz, Leann Marissa David from 6 Opal and Tan Sin Un from 5 Sapphire.

Ann Photographic Exhibition 2026 (NAPE).</p>
<p>The exhibition will be held at Gallery 1, LASALLE College of the Arts,
1 McNally St, Singapore 187940, from 13 June, Saturday, to 23 June 2026,
Tuesday&nbsp; (closed on Sundays).</p>
