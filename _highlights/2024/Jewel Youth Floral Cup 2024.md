<p>Well done to the 12 students who&nbsp;participated in Jewel Youth Floral
Cup 2024 at Jewel Changi Airport on 24 May 2024.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Congratulations to the following students for their achievements:</p>
<p>Teo Kai Ting (P4 Sapphire) - Bronze Award (Category B)</p>
<p>Judith Seow (P6 Topaz) - Merit Award (Category B)</p>
<p>Jolyn Seow (P3 Topaz) - Merit Award (Category B)</p>
