<p>On 21 November, we were delighted to welcome our 2026 Primary 1 cohort
for their very first visit to the school. It was a lively and exciting
morning as our new students began their orientation experience.</p>
<p>The programme started with a Big Book reading session designed to ease
the children into the classroom environment while giving them a glimpse
of literacy learning in primary school. Through the stories, they explored
meaningful themes such as the importance of listening and learning from
others. The morning continued with cheerful teamwork as students took part
in hula hoop games that encouraged cooperation, respect, and empathy.</p>
<p>Our teachers also had the opportunity to get to know the children better
through a simple check-in activity, where students shared their feelings
and interests.</p>
<p>While the children engaged in classroom activities, their parents attended
informative sessions conducted by our School Leaders and partners to support
a smooth transition into Primary 1.</p>
<p>The orientation concluded with a food-tasting of the central kitchen meals
prepared by our vendor, Gourmetz. It was a warm and engaging start to the
journey ahead, and we look forward to welcoming our new Primary 1 students
in 2026.</p>
