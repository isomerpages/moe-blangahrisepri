<p>Congratulations to Kayleigh from Primary 6 Topaz, MediaKids CCA.&nbsp;
Her photograph titled “Follow Your Passion!” is shortlisted for exhibition
at the 21<sup>st</sup>&nbsp;Ngee Ann Photographic Exhibition 2026 (NAPE).</p>
<p>The exhibition will be held at Gallery 1, LASALLE College of the Arts,
1 McNally St, Singapore 187940, from 13 June, Saturday, to 23 June 2026,
Tuesday&nbsp; (closed on Sundays).</p>
