<p>Our Primary 3 students had an amazing experience at Ngee Ann Polytechnic
- School of Film and Media Studies! They got to explore the impressive
facilities and learn how professional equipment is used in the media industry.
In addition, they had an opportunity to experience how it would be like
to read their lines on air. This trip was a fantastic opportunity for our
students to gain valuable insights about the media industry and develop
essential 21st Century Competencies.</p>
