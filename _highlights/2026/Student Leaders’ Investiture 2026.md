<h4><strong>Student Leaders’ Investiture 2026</strong></h4>
<p>On 3 February, our school proudly held the Student Leaders’ Investiture,
marking a significant milestone in the leadership journey of our students.
During this meaningful ceremony, we recognised the leadership qualities
and achievements of 61 student leaders, comprising our Prefects, CCA Leaders
and Peer Support Leaders, who have stepped forward to serve and lead our
school community.</p>

grace the ceremony and personally pin the leadership badge on their child.
This symbolic gesture reflected the strong partnership between home and
school in nurturing confident and values-driven leaders.</p>

<p>As our Student Leaders begin their roles, they are encouraged to lead
by example and uphold our RISE values - Respect, Integrity, Self-discipline
and Empathy - in all that they do.</p>
<p>We extend our heartfelt congratulations to all our Student Leaders. We
look forward to seeing them grow in confidence, inspire their peers, and
lead with heart and purpose.</p>
