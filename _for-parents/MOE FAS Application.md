<p>Dear Parent/Guardian,</p>
<p>The MOE FAS is to help financially needy Singaporean students in Government
and Government-aided schools to meet basic schooling expenses. Singapore
Citizen students from households with gross monthly household income (GHI)
of $4,000 and below, or per capita income (PCI) of $1,000 and below will
be eligible for the MOE FAS.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2025 Photo Album/FAS.jpg">
</div>
<p>Please <strong>fill out the <a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-efasggas" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">online 2026 MOE FAS application form</a></strong> by <strong>7 November 2025</strong> if
you wish to apply for financial assistance.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Upon successful application, you may also apply for the <strong><a href="https://eservice.imda.gov.sg/das/homepage" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Info-communications Media Development Authority’s (IMDA) NEU PC Plus Programme</a></strong>,
which offers students the opportunity to own a new computer at an affordable
price.</p>
<p></p>
<p>If you have any enquiries, please contact the school’s General Office
at <strong>6721 7387</strong>.</p>
