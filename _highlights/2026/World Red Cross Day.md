<p>Our school commemorated World Red Cross Day through a series of meaningful
and engaging activities aimed at raising awareness of the humanitarian
efforts of the Red Cross Movement.</p>
<p>The commemoration began with the sharing of the World Red Cross Day message,
reminding students of the importance of compassion, kindness, and service
to others.</p>
<p>Our Red Cross members took on active leadership roles by facilitating
interactive recess activities and organizing a mini exhibition featuring
informative posters on the history and humanitarian work of the Red Cross.</p>
<p>Through the various hands-on activities and displays, students gained
a deeper appreciation of the importance of helping those in need and contributing
positively to the community. The event provided an enriching and memorable
learning experience that reinforced the values of empathy, care, and active
citizenship.</p>
