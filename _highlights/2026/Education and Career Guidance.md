<h4><strong>Education and Career Guidance</strong></h4>
<p>Through our recent Education and Career Guidance (ECG) activities, our
students were given meaningful opportunities to discover more about themselves,
explore various career pathways, and understand the importance of lifelong
learning.</p>
<p>The programme began with an inspiring sharing session by our school alumna,
Ms Janice Lee, who fondly recalled her days as a student at Blangah Rise
Primary School and shared her journey as a Human Resources professional.
Her story encouraged students to adopt a growth mindset, remain resilient,
and make thoughtful choices as they shape their own future pathways.</p>
<p>Our Lower Primary students also took the stage to share their passions
and strengths, helping them build confidence and self-awareness as they
reflected on their interests and aspirations. The highlight of the programme
was the Careers Day Fair, where parent volunteers set up interactive booths
and engaged students by sharing insights into their professions and real-world
working experiences.</p>
<p>Parent volunteer Ms Shauna Xie, a Regional Finance Business Partner, shared,</p>
<p>“I had a wonderful time with the students. I could see strong interest
from many of them — perhaps some future cabin crew, pilots, or aerospace
engineers in the making, especially among those curious about my role.
It was also encouraging to see how many students spoke up confidently and
were eager to ask questions. This is a meaningful annual event that gives
students valuable insight into working life and the wide variety of careers
available.”</p>
<p>Students also shared how the ECG activities inspired them. Kayleigh from
6 Topaz shared that she enjoyed Ms Janice Lee’s alumni talk and was intrigued
by the evolution of her career journey. She learnt the importance of perseverance
and choosing one’s own pathway, and especially enjoyed hearing the tune
of the old school song once again. Anaya from 4 Topaz shared,</p>
<p>“I learnt that you can be anything you want to be when you grow up as
long as you do not give up. I hope I will have opportunities to try and
experience different jobs in the future.”</p>
<p>Through these ECG experiences, our students learnt that every occupation
plays an important role in society and that their interests, strengths,
and choices can enable them to contribute meaningfully to the community.
As they continue to discover their purpose, explore opportunities, and
stay future-ready, we hope they will grow into confident, adaptable, and
resilient learners prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.</p>
