<p>Every year, the President’s Award for Teachers (PAT) and the Outstanding
Youth in Education Award (OYEA) are given out to teachers in Singapore
to recognise their professional excellence.&nbsp; Both PAT and OYEA are
national awards. Nominations for PAT 2026 and OYEA 2026 are now open and
will close on Friday, 9 January 2026. We would like to invite you and your
child/ward to submit your nomination for one or more deserving teachers.
Your nomination will be a source of encouragement for the teachers.</p>
