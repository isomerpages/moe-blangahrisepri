<p><strong>P4 Student wins “I Can Be a Caring Commuter Hero!” Merit Awar</strong>d</p><p>Leia Lay, a 4 Opal student, was honored with a Certificate of Merit for her exceptional achievement in the "I Can Be a Caring Commuter Hero!" art competition organized by the Land Transport Authority. Her recognition comes as a testament to her remarkable talent and creativity, which have had a significant impact by inspiring others to become more considerate commuters and fostering a sense of community within public transport.</p><p>Her artwork was showcased at the Caring Commuter Week Carnival at Punggol One in 2023. Starting from 31 January, Leia’s artwork will be featured at Buona Vista MRT Station (EWL).
